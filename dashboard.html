Exemption Types
Select which SCA exemptions Yuno should request when applicable

Low Value ?

Transactions below €30 (EU) / £25 (UK). Cumulative limit: €100 or 5 transactions.

AUTO-DETECTED

Recurring Payment ?

Subsequent payments in a recurring series with fixed amount and same payee. First payment must be SCA-authenticated.

AUTO-DETECTED

Transaction Risk Analysis ?

Real-time risk scoring indicates low fraud probability. Requires a fraud provider (Forter, Riskified) in your route.

REQUIRES FRAUD PROVIDER

Trusted Beneficiary ?

Cardholder has whitelisted your merchant with their issuing bank. Issuer decides approval.

ISSUER-SIDE

Secure Corporate ?

Payments made with corporate cards via dedicated, secured business processes and protocols.

MERCHANT DECLARATION

Delegated Authentication ?

Authentication delegated to a trusted third party. Available for merchants with their own SCA capability.
